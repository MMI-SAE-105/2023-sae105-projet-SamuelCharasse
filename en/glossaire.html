Glossary

Autocracy: Autocracy is a system of government in which one person holds absolute and uncontrolled power, making unilateral decisions without the consultation or consent of the people.

Thriller: A thriller is a literary or film genre characterised by an intense and gripping plot, often centred on suspense, danger, and elements of psychological tension. Thrillers seek to maintain the audience’s excitement and anticipation throughout the story.

Conformity: Conformity refers to the act of complying with established social norms, rules or expectations, frequently under pressure from the group or authority, even if this goes against one’s own convictions or personal judgement.
